Where it started:

My name is Nakayla and I am 24 years old. I got into web development because of my husband who suggested I try out a python programming class my second year in collage. At first I was against it since it didn't go with my food science degree, but ever since that class, I'e loved the whole programming industry since. I didn't want to start a lot of my schooling over again so I decided to take this bootcamp in hopes of starting off my career in tech sooner.

I am forever grateful for my number one supporter in everything, my husband, who has never given up on me. No matter how poorly or how great I was doing in school or life. He has always been there to support me and my dreams and helping me grow into the healthy woman I am today.

I love being outdoors! Hiking, traveling, my favorite place to those things so far has been the redwood/sequia forest in California and Oregon. I love food, so anytime I can cook or go out to eat I'm ALWAYS excited!
